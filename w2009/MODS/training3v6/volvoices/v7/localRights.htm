On Page 6 of the DLC-MODS Workbook, there is a link Click here for more information. about Use and Reproduction.

Currently this link goes to this page (localRights.htm), which you are welcome to revise for your own purposes.

But your Institution may already have a page that describes the rights for Digital Library Documents. If that page exists, revise Page 6 of the DLC-MODS Workbook so that the link goes to your official rights page.

Be sure to also change the value of the hidden form variable "use_and_reproduction" on Page 6.

An example of a Rights page is shown below.

Your Institution: Rights Statement

This work is the property of your institution, city, state.

It may be used freely by individuals for research, teaching, and personal use as long as this statement of availability is included in the text.

For all use contact: name, address, phone, email.
