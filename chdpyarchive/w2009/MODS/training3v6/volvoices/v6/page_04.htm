The Subject represents the primary topic(s) upon which a work is focused.
When using Library of Congress Subject Headings, separate each facet with double dashes, leaving a space on both sides of the double dashes.
Example: Baseball -- Tennessee -- Knoxville (NOT Baseball--Tennessee--Knoxville)
