MODS LABEL VALUE   
Subject


  • Represents the primary topic(s) on which a work is focused.  


  • Subjects are broken into four types.  

    ---Topical: Subject terms that do not fit into the three categories listed below 
    (geographic, temporal, name).


    ---Geographic: Geographic subject terms.

    ---Temporal: Chronological subject terms or temporal coverage.

    ---Name: A name used as a subject.



  • At least one subject (topical, geographic, temporal, or name) is required.



When using Library of Congress Subject Headings, 
    separate each facet with double dashes, 
    leaving a space on both sides of the double dashes.

    
Example: Baseball -- Tennessee -- Knoxville (NOT Baseball--Tennessee--Knoxville)
