<physicalDescription>

  • Summary of Requirements for <physicalDescription>
    The DLF/Aquifer Implementation Guidelines for Shareable MODS Records require the use of one <physicalDescription> element, one subelement <digitalOrigin>, and at least one subelement <internetMediaType>. This element is not repeatable. (page 39)
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 2: At least one <physicalDescription> element with <internetMediaType> subelement; value to be taken from appropriate list
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 3: <form> subelement of <physicalDescription> with @authority attribute
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 4: <digitalOrigin> subelement of <physicalDescription>
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 4: @authority attributes in general
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 5: <note> subelement within , when applicable
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 5: @authority attribute on subelement , when applicable
