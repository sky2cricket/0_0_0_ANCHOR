      <pb ref="0032_000051_000200_0001" seq="01" n="1"/>
       <p>Middle Tennessee Giles Co Dier Brother Sir i Drop you a fiew lines to let you Know how we are getting a long we are getting a long very slow, thogough we are all well and have been we will since we left your bounty, we have lost some four days all ready one the account of Hickney in Mr Blackimores family has to day on the Account of a sick Child. it is very bad, he don&apos;t way me to leave him and i don&apos;t want to leave him for One weeke yiet if he cant trabel in that time i will go on, we have had good luck not one thing rong with any of my stoic But scalded sholders are a little, Our youngest child was very poorly when we left but has improved all the time is getting fat Our flokes infors the trip before very well, Roads is good not much Kain since we started expereinces is very high the lehapist leome we got was 60 cts the dearist was $ 1.78 an ego about 75 cts avrg pr day is about $3.00 is takes money very fast to feed six head of horses &amp; twelve in the family, we have Been on pke Roads pikege is very experience, it will least me some $300.00 Dollars fo grain and provisions is as Dear from here On as from here Back, On the Cumberland Mountain the thieves tried to steal our horses, But our vigilance kept them from gettin any one of the thieves for his hand on our horse But was seen Before could cut the halter But could not shoot at him for fear of killing a horse we kep Sentinill every nisht for five nights David Norington Burstid a cap at one of them Sais if his gun had fired he would have killed him Dead i took too fires with a shot gun Dont know whether i hit him of not was Determined to kill if I could we have heard since we got off the mountain there was horses stolen from iners allso heard that some mover had shot a man on the mountain God send the movers would kill half the mountain men for they are nothing but thieves and robers, </p>
      <pb ref="0032_000051_000200_0002" seq="02" n="2"/>
       <p>in closed i send a ten Dollar Bill Back that i Got from Thomas J Wilson on Saturday Before i left on Monday never Noticed teh Bill at the time i got it from him, i send it to you with the hissisary Probet which i want you to see him and leans him to pay you good money fors the same if it is too much trouble for you to go down to see him you will leave the probet with Col Crawford or Collect it and have it ready to send to me when you get more Ready to send me i will not wright much at this time give our respects to susanan &amp; jackson Kelpper &amp; REbecca Mead and all that inquires abot us tell col Crawford that we are well &amp; so is Isaac Eunnist i write on my Knee the children a Squaling Round the Boys a Shooting all is life in Our Camp. Tell Richard Worthington that David is well seens to injoy the trip very well i am well pleased with the Company, this the 10 of Oct 1859 Ps i will wright to you often when i get to my Jouneys End Yours Truly Benj Bowman</p>
