Not trashed necessarily, but it’s certainly not going to be enhanced anytime soon. The library has no plans to hire anyone with any archival training anytime in the near future. Special Collections will be operating with the head, Nick, and Bill, as both Hannah and Elizabeth are leaving sometime soon as well. The library saw no need to do an external search for the head position, instead relying on internal candidates, neither of whom have any archival processing, reference, or management experience at all. There is no plan in place to hire a university archivist replacement, and it’s highly unlikely that Hannah or Elizabeth’s positions would be filled anytime soon.

 

I personally asked Rita if Special Collections would ever be given the support it needed to be successful and to do “new” things, like looking at ways to deal with born-digital records. She flat out said “no” and “I don’t think Special Collections will ever be an emphasis in the library.” Those were her exact words. I spoke to Barbara, and, while she did not say anything specific, she said nothing to contradict Rita’s statements. I don’t want to work in an environment where my contributions don’t matter.
