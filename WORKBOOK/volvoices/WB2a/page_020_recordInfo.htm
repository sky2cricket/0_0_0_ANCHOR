<recordInfo>

  • Summary of Requirements for <recordInfo>
    The DLF/Aquifer Implementation Guidelines for Shareable MODS Records require the use of one <recordInfo> element with the subelement <languageOfCataloging> to record the language of the text in the MODS record. <recordInfo> is not repeatable. (page 93)
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 4: one <recordInfo> element with the subelement <languageOfCataloging> to record the language of the text in the MODS record
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 4: @authority attributes in general
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 5: <recordContentSource> element, with @authority attribute, in <recordInfo>
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 5: @source attribute for <recordInfo>/<recordIdentifier>, when present
  • Per the Levels of Adoption Document Level 5: <recordOrigin> in <recordInfo>
  • Required Fields have yellow-highlighted input box.
