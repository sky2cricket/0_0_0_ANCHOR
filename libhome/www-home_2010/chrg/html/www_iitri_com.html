Commitment to Excellence

Founded in 1936, IITRI is one of the largest not-for-profit contract research and development institutes in the U.S.

Headquartered in Chicago and affiliated with the Illinois Institute of Technology, IITRI provides state-of-the-art research and development services to government and industry in the areas of electromagnetic engineering, information technology, life sciences, manufacturing and chemical technologies, and transportation systems.
