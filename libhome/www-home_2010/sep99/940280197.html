My unit is looking to hire a talented student to work 10 to 20 hours
a week to help us grow our web operations. The position would be for
the remainder of fall semester with the potential of continuing into
future semesters depending on interest and ability.

We're looking for someone with experience in basic web publishing and
comfort in working on self-directed tasks. Client platforms are Mac
with some access to PCs with various softare packages used.
Candidates need to be able to take text files and tabular material
and efficiently convert them into HTML. Prowess in developing web
graphics or at least familiarity with basic Photoshop functions or
other image software is preferred. Ability to do high quality work,
including some editing or altering of words without adding errors, is
a must.

We offer flexibility in scheduling work hours and a creative and
interesting work environment. Tasks can be discrete and result into
projects that will look good cited on your resume.
Please contact me if you're interested.
