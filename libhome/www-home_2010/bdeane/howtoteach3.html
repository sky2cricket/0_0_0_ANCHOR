A teacher only succeeds when the student truly understands. The challenge for
the computer science teacher is to explain complex technologies in a
comprehensible manner. While the overall needs of the class must be met, the
more advanced students must be challenged with the opportunity to learn beyond
the boundaries of the course and those who lag behind must be offered both
encouragement and additional help. Equally important is the need to project
a desire to learn into each student by fostering a positive and interesting
classroom environment. Ideally, the class will be the high point of each
student's day.
