All of my life I have enjoyed teaching and explaining. Nothing else I have ever

done has brought me more long term satisfaction. At every job I have ever held,

I have instinctively gravitated towards assuming teaching duties and writing

training manuals. The explosion in computer technology that has occurred over

the past few decades is just a portent of what the future holds. Teaching at

Pellissippi State will allow me to be part of that future and to affect the long

term well-being of our community in a most beneficial manner.
