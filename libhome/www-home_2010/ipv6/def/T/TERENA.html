Trans-European Research and Education Networking Association (TERENA)

TERENA was formed in October 1994 by the merger of RARE and EARN to promote and participate in the development of a high quality international information and telecommunications infrastructure for the benefit of research and education. See also: Reseaux Associes pour la Recherche Europeen, European Academic and Research Network. [Source: TERENA Statutes] (From RFC1983.)
