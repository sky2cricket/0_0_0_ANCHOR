Lurking

No active participation on the part of a subscriber to a mailing list or USENET newsgroup. A person who is lurking is just listening to the discusssion. Lurking is encouraged for beginners who need to get up to speed on the history of the group. See also: Electronic Mail, mailing list, Usenet. [Source: LAQUEY] (From RFC1983.)
