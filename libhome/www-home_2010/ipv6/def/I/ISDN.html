Integrated Services Digital Network (ISDN)

An emerging technology which is beginning to be offered by the telephone carriers of the world. ISDN combines voice and digital network services in a single medium, making it possible to offer customers digital data services as well as voice connections through a single "wire." The standards that define ISDN are specified by CCITT. See also: CCITT. [Source: RFC1208] (From RFC1983.)

Integrated Services Digital Network. This service will provide digital telecommunications world wide for video, audio, and digital data communications.
