mailing list

A list of email addresses, used by a mail exploder, to forward messages to groups of people. Generally, a mailing ist is used to discuss certain set of topics, and different mailing lists discuss different topics. A mailing list may be moderated. This means that messages sent to the list are actually sent to a moderator who determinins whether or not to send the messages on to everyone else. Requests to subscribe to, or leave, a mailing list should ALWAYS be sent to the lists's "-request" address (e.g., ietf-request@cnri.reston.va.us for the IETF mailing list) or majordomo server. See also: Electronic Mail, mail exploder, email address, moderator, majordomo. (From RFC1983.)
