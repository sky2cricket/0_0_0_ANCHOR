American National Standards Institute (ANSI)

This organization is responsible for approving U.S. standards in many areas, including computers and communications. Standards approved by this organization are often called ANSI standards (e.g., ANSI C is the version of the C language approved by ANSI). ANSI is a member of ISO. See also International Organization for Standardization. [Source: NNSC] (From RFC1983.)
