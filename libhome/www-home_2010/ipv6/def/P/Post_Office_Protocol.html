Post Office Protocol

A protocol designed to allow single user hosts to read electronic mail from a server. Version 3, the most recent and most widely used is defined in RFC1725. (Sometimes the acronym POP is used to stand for Post Office Protocol, but it is better to used POP only for "Point Of Presence.") See also: Electronic Mail. (From RFC1983.)
