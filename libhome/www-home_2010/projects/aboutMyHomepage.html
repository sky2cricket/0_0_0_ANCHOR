About My Homepage by Cricket Haygood Deane
Last fall someone doing statistics on Internet Traffic reported to me that my homepage is one of the most heavily visited sites in the CS Department at UTK.

It's not because of the artwork!

It may be the tutorials or the links or the fact that the CS100 lab manual still sends students to my page.

I have gone for a fairly minimal look, just to make the download faster. You can find links to many of my other projects and tutorials there. I also have a little embedded javascript to send a greeting that varies with the time of day.
