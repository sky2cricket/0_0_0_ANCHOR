CS100 Homepage Guide by Cricket Haygood Deane
and course management scripts in AWK
The Homepage Guide is a tutuorial for HTML written in 1996 which is still being used in the course CS100 taught in the CS Department at UTK. Over 2000 people have learned to use HTML from this tutorial while enrolled in CS100. I have received comments from many other folks not in CS100 who have also used this tutorial.

The Guide is part of a full course development package. I developed a suite of online pages for the course, and wrote a lab manual that provided print interface for web-based materials.

The AWK scripts included software to generate class attendance sheets, grading sheets, and web pages from standard UTK electronic classroll data. Most of this software is still in use.
