I would like to apply for the Online Producer position.

I earned my Masters in Computer Science at the University of Tennessee. I have worked with a wide variety of computers all of my life and I have a very comprehensive background in IT and web site development, especially with regard to creating and displaying reference material.

I helped develop the DevGuru Web Site (http://www.devguru.com), which is one of the most successful reference sources ever created for the Internet. The DevGuru site currently enjoys well over 170 million hits and well over 36 million page views per year.

The purpose of DevGuru is to provide the Web Designer with comprehensive information about the computer languages and programming techniques used to create web sites on the Internet. I created all, or the major portions, of the extensive Quick References for the following computer languages: ADO, ASP, CSS2, HTML, Jet SQL, VBScript, WML, WMLScript, XHTML, and XSLT. These are the languages that serve as the backbone of the Internet. For each language, the Quick Reference explains the syntax and grammar, and provides useful, working examples which I created and tested.

I can construct web sites using both original code and standard development tools, such as Dreamweaver, Homesite, and Photoshop.

I am a very accomplished photographer. My clients have included Time-Life, Sierra Club, National Park Service, and the National Geographic. I have worked with a variety of formats, ranging from 35mm up to 8"x10", 16mm movie, and digital. I also have experience on both sides of the TV camera.

I enjoy interacting with people and I pride myself on my positive, patient demeanor.

You have much to gain by adding me to your staff.
