MSDOS 

Setting the Serial Communications Port
The format is 


        MODE COMn:baud,parity,databits,stopbits,P

        where n    is either 1 or 2
              baud is 110, 150, 300, 600, 1200, 2400, 4800,9600
              parity is N, O or E (default)
              databits is 7 (default) or 8
              stopbits is 1 (default if baud != 110)
                         or  2 (baud = 110)

              P indicates that the serial port is being used with a serial printer.
              If P is specified, the computer will continuously re-try timeout errors.
