white pages

The Internet supports several databases that contain basic information about users, such as e-mail addresses, telephone numbers, and postal addresses. These databases can be searched to get information about particular individuals. Because they serve a funciton akin to the telephone book, these databases are often refered to as "white pages." See also: Knowbot, netfind, whois, X.500, InterNIC. (From RFC1983.)
