Comite Consultatif International de Telegraphique et Telephonique (CCITT)

This organization is now part of the International Telecommunications Union and is responsible for making technical recommendations about telephone and data communications systems. Every four years CCITT holds plenary sessions where they adopy new standards; the most recent was in 1992. Recently, the ITU reorganized and CCITT was renamed ITU-TSS. See also: International Telecommunications Union - Telecommunications Standards Sector. (From RFC1983.)
