
Christine Haygood Deane, M.S. is a Senior Research Associate/Webmaster for
the CHRG.  She holds a B.S. in Geology from the University of Tennessee,
Knoxville, an M.S. in Geochemistry from the Pennsylvania State University, 
and an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville.
She spent over 14 years with the Tennessee Valley Authority coordinating
data collection for geologic map production.  Her computer skils include 
teaching, online tutorials, interactive web page design, and scientific 
programming.  She provides guidance in the areas of website integration
and functionality.
